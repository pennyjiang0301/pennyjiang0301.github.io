I received my B.S. from Southeast University, China in 2010. Now, I am a PhD candidate supervised by Prof Qiaoyan Wen in the Department of State Key Laboratory of Networking and Switch Technology, Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications, China. During 2014 to 2015, I was a visiting student working with Prof Yi Mu and Dr Fuchun Guo at the Institute of Cybersecurity and Cryptology, School of Computing and Information Technology, University of Wollongong, Australia. My research interests include public key cryptography, applied cryptography and information security.
